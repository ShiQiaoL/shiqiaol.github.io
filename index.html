Xiaoqing Liu

Postdoctoral Researcher, Department of Mechanical Engineering, National University of Singapore

I work on atomistic modeling, machine-learned interatomic potentials, and data-driven computational workflows for energy materials.

I am interested in developing reliable AI-enabled tools for catalysts, battery interfaces, and complex functional materials. My research combines density functional theory, molecular simulation, and model fine-tuning to improve both physical fidelity and practical usability.
